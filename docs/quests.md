# Quests

The app supports classroom/manual quests and Lichess Quest Rules.

Teachers manage ordinary quests at `/admin/quests` and Lichess activity conditions at `/admin/quests/lichess-rules`. Detected Lichess completions are reviewed at `/admin/lichess/quest-awards`.

Students see live quests and their private Lichess progress at `/student/quests`. The detailed progress view is `/student/lichess-progress`.
